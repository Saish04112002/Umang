Bachelors in Business Administration

BBA Retail Management

Retail Management is the method of handling the retail of products to retail store customers for personal use. It involves all the steps involved in getting customers into the store, addressing the shopping needs of customers, saving time and maintaining customer satisfaction.

About this Course

Marketing careers include personal sales in retail and commercial environments, new product planning for major marketers, consulting organisations, and advertising agencies in manufacturing firms and service providers, and marketing research. Marketing specialisation courses concentrate on particular marketing subjects, such as marketing analysis, advertisement, personal sales and delivery across networks. Students who have successfully completed the programme for this major would have gained expertise in the application of marketing theory and have achieved maturity in analytical reasoning, quantitative research, and oral and written communication objectives for general education.

Subjects

The course curriculum of BBA in Retail Management is so designed as to give a student a top-to-bottom knowledge fo the functioning and supervision of a retail establishment. Students learn key concepts such as marketing, customer relationship management, strategies used in retailing, human resource management etc.

Jobs and Recruitments

In India, there is a huge growth rate for urban as well as rural retailing. It is therefore very evident that this huge industry offers professionals in retail management limitless job opportunities and a vast range of growth.
The average annual course fee charged in India ranges between INR 9,000 to 7 lakhs, depending upon the institute offering the course.
