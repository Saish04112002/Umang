Bachelors in Business Administration

BBA Finance

B.B.A. Finance or Bachelor of Business Administration in Finance is a three-year undergraduate program in financial management

About this Course

In every corporation and organisation, the finance department is of tremendous importance in handling their expenditures and taxes. Students are good with numbers and may opt for this course if they are interested in managing the finances of any company. The primary aim of the BBA in Finance programme is to train students in finance for entry-level profiles. It also develops some necessary skills, such as investment and insurance, required for financial management. Financial management is all about preserving and assessing the costs of different government and private businesses.
